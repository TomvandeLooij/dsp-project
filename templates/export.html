Calculating the scores

When a building is on fire, there can be multiple infrastructural entities in jeopardy, like buildings, roads and public transport. To give a building a score for how much danger they present for their surrounding infrastucture, different aspects have been quantified. Every different type of building, type of road and type of public transport. The scores that the different type of entities hold is given in the tables. So to calculate the score, the amount of times a certain entity occurs is multiplied with the corresponding weight. This is done for all entities in the radius and summed up to the visible score in the prototype.
